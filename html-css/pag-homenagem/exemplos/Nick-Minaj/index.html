Nicki Minaj Life and Career

  • Onika Tanya Maraj-Petty (born December 8, 1982), known professionally as Nicki Minaj (/mɪˈnɑːʒ/), is a Trinidadian-born American rapper, singer, and songwriter.
  • She was first spotted by the CEO of Young Money, and was later recruited for The Carter Edition of Young Money's own "The Come Up" DVD series. Her rapping skills caught the eyes of Lil Wayne who later worked with her for many collaborations with his mixtapes.
  • In April 2007, Minaj released her first mixtape "Playtime Is Over". One year later she made another mixtape "Sucka Free" which made her Female Artist of the Year at the Underground Music Awards. In 2009 she made her third mixtape "Beam Me Up Scotty" which got positive reviews from BET and MTV. To date, Nicki has released 3 platinum selling studio albums, Pink Friday, Pink Friday: Roman Reloaded, and the most recent The Pinkprint

Nicki Minaj Personal Life

Minaj married Petty in October 2019. In July 2020, she announced that she's expecting. Minaj and Petty welcomed their first child, a son, on September 30, 2020. Minaj previously dated rappers Meek Mill and Safaree Samuels.

Nicki Minaj Well Known Rumours

She Copied Lil' Kim When Nicki Minaj first broke out on the scene back in 2010 with her hit song 'Super Bass', she instantly skyrocketed to international success. As her career progressed, she was being compared more and more to rap iconic, Lil' Kim, and has been accused of copying her well throughout her career, which we are certain Nicki has had enough of.

Her Hatred For Mariah Carey Nicki Minaj has been accused of not getting along with a number of people in the industry, and one of those people is with the legendary Mariah Carey. Nicki and Mariah both worked on Season 12 of 'American Idol' together, and had a huge blow up on the show, however, Nicki made it clear that they do not hate one another, and simply had a tiff that was blown out of proportion by the media.

Huge Feud With Cardi B It appears Nicki Minaj has been pitted against a number of other female artists in the industry. In addition to Mariah Carey and Lil' Kim, Nicki has also been accused of having it out for Cardi B. Now the two may not be friends, but they certainly do not hate one another. Nicki declared there is "nothing but love", between the two and they both wish the other nothing but success.
